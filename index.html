LIONEL GARCIA



Astronomer & Research Fellow at CCA (Flatiron Institute, New York).

I develop computational methods and software to study exoplanets and their stars.

My research focuses on the smallest and coolest stars, called M dwarfs, and the variations in the light we receive from them (sometimes a planet).

Highlights

  • My work is open-source!
  • I participate in the operatation and maintainance of the SPECULOOS telescopes.
  • My collaborators and I will be observing a giant stellar spot with the JWST.
  • I work with theorists to understand what cool stars might look like.
  • I love teaching.

Software

    jaxoplanet: analytical models of stellar light curves powered by JAX (code)
    nuance: efficient detection of planets transiting active stars (code, paper)
    prose: modular image processing pipelines for Astronomy (code, paper)
    twirl: local astrometric plate solving in Python (code)
    spotter: approximate fluxes and spectra of non-uniform stars (code)
